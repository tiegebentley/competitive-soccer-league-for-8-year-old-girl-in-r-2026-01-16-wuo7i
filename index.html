About Carolina Velocity

Learn about our commitment to youth soccer excellence

Our Story

Carolina Velocity was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide young athletes in Raleigh, North Carolina with access to world-class competitive soccer training. What started as a small grassroots program has grown into one of the region's premier youth soccer organizations, serving hundreds of players each season. Our founders believed that every young athlete deserves the opportunity to develop their skills in a supportive, professional environment that emphasizes both competitive excellence and personal growth.

Over the past decade, we've built a reputation for developing not just better soccer players, but better young people. Our alumni have gone on to play at the collegiate level, earn academic scholarships, and become leaders in their communities. We're proud of the impact we've made and remain dedicated to nurturing the next generation of athletes.

Our Mission & Values

At Carolina Velocity, our mission is to empower young female athletes through competitive soccer, fostering excellence, confidence, and lifelong friendships. We believe in the transformative power of sports and are committed to creating an inclusive environment where every player can thrive. Our core values include integrity, teamwork, continuous improvement, and a passion for the beautiful game.

We envision a future where every young girl in Raleigh has access to quality coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and the opportunity to compete at the highest levels. We're not just developing soccer players—we're building confident, resilient young women who will make a positive impact in their communities long after they hang up their cleats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our program

Carolina Velocity offers competitive soccer programs for girls ages 6 through 18. Our 8-year-old program is one of our most popular divisions, with multiple teams at different competitive levels to ensure each player is appropriately challenged. We also offer programs for younger and older age groups, allowing players to grow with us throughout their youth soccer journey.

Our competitive program includes twice-weekly training sessions with professional coaches, competitive match play during the season, team uniforms, access to our training facilities, and participation in league tournaments. Pricing varies depending on the team level and season length. We offer flexible payment plans and financial assistance options for families who need them. Contact us for a detailed pricing quote and to discuss any questions about program costs.

While our competitive program is designed for players with some soccer experience, we welcome players of all skill levels. We have teams at different competitive levels, so beginners are placed with other developing players and receive appropriate coaching for their level. Our coaches are skilled at working with players at all stages of development and will help your daughter improve at her own pace while still providing competitive challenges.

We offer both fall and spring competitive seasons, each running approximately 10-12 weeks. The fall season typically runs from August through October, while the spring season runs from March through May. We also offer summer camps and training opportunities for players who want to continue developing during the off-season. Players can register for one season or commit to both seasons. Contact us for specific dates and registration deadlines for the upcoming season.
